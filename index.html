About Us

The Rotaract Club of Women's Christian College is a vibrant community of student leaders dedicated to creating positive change through service and leadership. Guided by the values of Rotary International, our members work together to build meaningful impact within the college and the wider community.

Our Legacy

Chartered on 10 May 1995, the Rotaract Club of Women's Christian College has continued to nurture generations of changemakers. Through social initiatives, community outreach programs, leadership development activities, and collaborative projects, the club has established a lasting legacy of service and excellence.
